How to make you card work well with our markdown-ish thing:

  1. We use a kind of noise cancellation where small - seemingly insignificant words such as it and and even and. These words will basically be ignored, we will not ask a question regarding them.

  2. If there is a word we don't cancel, but should be removed you can surround word like so: this is -unimportant-

  3. If there is a word we cancel, but it as actually important you can surround word like so: +these+ +are+ actuall important

  4. When we ask for gaps to be filled, we ask for one word - you can have several words grouped with an underscore like so: word_one_and_two are class as one
All of the noise words: and, if, so, or, this, is, that, are, what, a, its, as, it, to, by, of, there, they, does, an, like, who, when, why

WARNING: This means that the characters +, -, _ cannot be used normally! For example thing - something to explain further would no longer work!
